Peripherals

Monitor

The monitor is one of the most important peripherals to consider when building a PC. Monitors come in a variety of styles. These styles include: Regular, Curved, Ultrawide, Gaming,etc. Also, monitors come in a variety of different resolutions and refresh rates. The resolution is the amount of pixels on the screen. The more pixels you have on the screen, the clearer the image. However, this also goes without saying, you will need a more powerful GPU for a higher resolution. Refresh rates are how many times the screen refreshes itself to change the image on the screen. The higher the refresh rate, the faster your application can run on the screen.

Keyboard and Mouse

The Keyboard and Mouse is another important peripheral to consider when buying or building a PC. Keyboard and Mice can come in different forms. First, do you want your keyboard/mouse to wireless or wired. Second, do you want your mouse to laser or trackball depending on how precise you want your cursor to be. Finally, are you interested in RGB lighting? This cool feature allows your mouse and keyboard to light up in different colors, which you can change with software from the manufacturer.
