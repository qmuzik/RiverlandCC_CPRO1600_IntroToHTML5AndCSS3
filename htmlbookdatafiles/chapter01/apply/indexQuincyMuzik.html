In order to plan a website there are many steps that must be followed in order to start. First step, your must think about the websites purpose and who will be its target audience. Second step, make sure the webpage can be viewed on multiple platforms such as desktops or mobile devices such as cell phones and tablets. Third step, you must make a site map and think about its wireframe. Fourth step, make the website have some beautiful graphics, an appropriate color pallet and has a readable font scheme. Fifth step, make the website accessable to viewers who might have a disability. And the Final step, plan the websites budget and establish a timeline for each part of the website to be completed by. Those steps will allow you to plan and hopefully create a successful website.
